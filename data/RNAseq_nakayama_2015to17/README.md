# README: Transcriptome Data

## Dataset

Genome-wide transcriptome analysis of the medaka (*Oryzias latipes*) brain
(ventral telencephalon, hypothalamus, and pituitary) sampled under outdoor
natural conditions over two years.

- GEO accession: [GSE234401](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE234401)

## Notes

- Group 1 and Group 2 are biological replicates.
- Low-quality sequencing reads were filtered prior to analysis.
